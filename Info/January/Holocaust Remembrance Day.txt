International Holocaust Remembrance Day, is an international memorial day on 27 January commemorating 
the tragedy of the Holocaust that occurred during the Second World War. It commemorates the genocide
 that resulted in the death of an estimated 6 million Jewish people, 5 million Slavs, 
3 million ethnic Poles, 200,000 Romani people, 250,000 mentally and physically disabled people, 
and 9,000 homosexual men by the Nazi regime and its collaborators
