Romantic Festival of Valentine's Day is celebrated with joy and verve in several 
countries around the world. Every year on February 14, those in love take opportunity 
of the occasion to reaffirm their love for sweethearts. Many people also express gratitude and 
love for teachers, friends, parents, siblings or any other person who is dear to them. Most popular Valentine's Day
 gifts exchanged between loved ones across the globe are cards, fresh flowers and chocolates.
